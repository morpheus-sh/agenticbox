Why businesses trust it

Built for compliance

Governance isn't a feature we added. It's the foundation the platform is built on.

01

Scoped Permissions

Agents can only do what you authorize. refund ≤ $50? Yes. delete accounts? No. Every action stays within boundaries you define — enforced by the platform, not by prompt engineering.

02

Full Audit Trail

Every action the agent takes is logged, attributed, and timestamped. When your compliance team asks "who did this and why," you have the answer. Exportable, searchable, tamper-proof.

03

Isolated Execution

Agents run in bounded containers — not on your production servers. They can't access systems you haven't explicitly connected. No lateral movement, no privilege escalation.

04

Vendor-Neutral

Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or your own self-hosted models. No vendor lock-in. Your agent infrastructure stays yours — run on your cloud or ours.
