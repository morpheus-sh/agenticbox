How it works

From idea to production in four steps

1 · Pick a use case → 2 · Connect your tools

Customer support, sales ops, IT, or finance. Each comes with a pre-configured agent template. Connect your helpdesk, CRM, database, or APIs — we handle the integrations.

Zendesk Salesforce Stripe Postgres Slack Intercom

3 · Set boundaries

Define what the agent can and cannot do. Plain language, enforced by the platform — not by prompts.

refund ≤ $50 → delete account

4 · Deploy — the agent runs in production with full governance

Every action logged, attributed, and timestamped. When your compliance team asks "who did this and why," you have the answer. Exportable, searchable, tamper-proof. The agent runs in an isolated container — not on your production servers. No lateral movement, no privilege escalation.
